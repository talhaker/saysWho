saysWho? Helps you collect and manage inspirational quotes at the touch of a button. You may than save them in your personal Inspiration Book, and refer to them at any time. Hit “Next” for instructions
To search for quotes, you may enter one of more tags/author names – followed by the “Search” button. Omitting the search criteria returns a random “surprise” quote
Alternatively, you may explore the Vitruvian Man – for a fun and intuitive way to search for quotes
